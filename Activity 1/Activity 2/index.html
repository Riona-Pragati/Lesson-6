Facts About Music

Mental & Emotional Health

Reduces stress and anxiety
Slows heart rate and lowers cortisol levels.

Improves mood
Releases dopamine (the "feel-good" hormone).

Helps with depression
Can provide emotional expression and relief.

Relaxation & Sleep

Promotes relaxation
Soothing music calms the nervous system.

Improves sleep quality
Reduces time to fall asleep and improves sleep depth.

Physical Health

Lowers blood pressure and heart rate

Supports pain management
Used in hospitals to reduce pain perception.

Improves physical performance
Music can boost stamina and motivation during exercise.

Cognitive Function

Enhances memory and concentration
Especially with classical or instrumental music.

Boosts creativity
Helps trigger imaginative thinking and problem-solving.

Learning & Academic Performance

Boosts creativity
Helps trigger imaginative thinking and problem-solving.

Improves focus while studying
Background music (especially instrumental) can help with sustained attention.

Supports language learning
Through repetition, rhythm, and melody.

Social Connection

Strengthens bonds
Shared music experiences promote connection.

Cultural expression and identity
Music reflects traditions, heritage, and social values.

"Music washes away from the soul the dust of everyday life."
